What is Triangular LeftValues?

Triangular LeftValues is a leftist political quiz inspired by the original LeftValues and 8values projects. Unlike traditional binary-axis quizzes, each category here uses a ternary plot — an equilateral triangle — where three positions compete simultaneously. Your answers place you within each triangle, giving a much richer picture of your political alignment.

There are 89 questions across 8 axes. Answer honestly!

What does 'Closest Match' mean?

At the end of the quiz, your scores are compared against 26 political ideologies using Euclidean distance. The ideology whose ideal profile is nearest to yours is shown as your closest match, along with 4 runner-up matches.
